// FAQ

Is this legal?

Yes. All information is gathered from publicly available sources. OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) is the same method used by law enforcement, media and professional investigation agencies.

Will the person know I checked them?

No. Since we only use open sources, no notification or tracking is sent to the person.

What do I get in the report?

A thorough PDF report with identity, address, companies, legal history, media mentions, digital profile and more. Everything with source references and confidence ratings.

How long does it take?

Identity verification: 24 hours. Background check and footprint: 48 hours. Rush delivery available by arrangement.

Can I use the report in court?

The report is an intelligence product, not a legal document. It can be used as a basis for further investigation or decisions, but is not legal evidence in itself.
