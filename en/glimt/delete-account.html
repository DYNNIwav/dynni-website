Delete account and data

Last updated: 23 June 2026

You can delete your Glimt account and all data linked to it at any time. This cannot be undone.

Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Glimt and go to Settings (the bear logo at the top-left of the camera).
  2. Scroll down to Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account is deleted immediately.

Request deletion without the app

If you can't access the app, email paal@dynni.no from the address your account is registered with, with the subject "Delete my Glimt account". We'll delete the account within 30 days and confirm when it's done.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email, username, avatar, date of birth).
  • Your friends and bål memberships.
  • All glimt and vekeglimt (these are ephemeral and end-to-end encrypted anyway).
  • Your push token and settings.

Reports with their evidence may be retained as long as needed to meet legal safety obligations, then deleted.
